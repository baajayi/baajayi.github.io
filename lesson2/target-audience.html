Target Audience

The target audience include people from all walks of life. Professionals, business owners, students, visitors, and tourists to Preston. They could be working in a news agency reporting weather or happenings in Preston. They could be managers hoping to have a fair weather to host a meeting, or a student looking for data for an assignment. They could be users of high speed internet provided by Wi-Fi technology or maybe the best they could use is a dial-up connection. everythingpreston.org will serve the needs of anyone who seek news, location, and weather information.

Persona 1

George Priestly is 40-year-old Construction Engineering Manager with ongoing projects scattered around town. He has met with site supervisors once a week for the past three weeks via zoom. Despite these meetings, the pace of work appeared to still be slow. He is contemplating inviting the supervisors to a face-to-face meeting next week but the unpredictability of the Preston weather is giving him concern. He uses the office Wi-Fi to access the internet during work hours and uses his phone internet after work and on weekends. He needs to know i. up to date weather information by the minute, ii. happenings in Preston by the minute, and iii. distance that each of his supervisors needs to travel to the meeting venue. He heads to everythingpreston.org

Persona 2

Carissa Holmes is a 23-year-old grade 1 teacher in downtown Preston. She has a certificate in Early Childhood Education and is passionate about showing things rather than telling her pupils about things. She has taught her pupils about some animals that can be found in the zoo. She wants to know i. if the zoo will be opened by next week Tuesday and ii. the weather conditions for that same day to detemine if it will be clement enough to go out with the kids. She basically uses her phone to access the internet all the time.
