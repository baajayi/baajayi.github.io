Target Audence

Scoot Island is targetted at tourism enthusiasts and entrepreneurs. THose who wishes to fulfill their adrenaline rush and those who get paid to help others do so. The age, employment, marital status or skin color do not matter. If you love fun and Coruzel Island is an option, then scootisland.net is where you should begin your adventure.
